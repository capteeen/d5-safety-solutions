What We Offer

Everything Your
Food Business
Needs

From on-site audits to digital HACCP systems — we cover every pillar of food safety compliance under one roof. Trusted by 500+ businesses across the UK.

6 Core Services
01

Food Safety Audits

Comprehensive on-site and remote audits by certified consultants. Identify risks before they become incidents — with full corrective action tracking.

 02

HACCP & Documentation

Fully digital HACCP plans, policies, and procedures. Auto-timestamped, photo-evidenced, and export-ready as PDF in a single tap.

 03

Staff Training & Certification

Online and in-person food hygiene training. Track certificates, expiry dates, and onboarding progress for every team member across all sites.

 04

Temperature Monitoring

Manual logging plus optional IoT sensor integration. Automated alerts fire instantly for any out-of-range readings — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

 05

Allergen Management

Digital allergen matrices, menu management, and real-time updates. Full Natasha's Law compliance built in from day one — zero compromise.

 06

Compliance Consulting

Dedicated food safety consultant support on demand. From EHO preparation to corrective action plans — we handle the complexity so you don't have to.
The Platform

One Platform.
Total Compliance.

The D5 app brings every compliance module together — accessible on mobile and desktop, online or offline. Built for busy food business operators.
